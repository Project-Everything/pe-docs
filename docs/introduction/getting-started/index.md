<b>Project Everything</b> is an expansive world-building project started in 2017, with an ever-growing collection of
lore, stories, characters, perceptions, and concepts that together form a massive melting pot of creativity. Anyone can
contribute to the project.

contributions that fall under the rules and regulations of this document or any other platforms are subject to being
used as canon lore to Project Everything. We ask that you credit ‘Project Everything’ if the material is used for any
product.
