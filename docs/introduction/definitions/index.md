# {{ $frontmatter.title }}

## The Everything

Project Everything revolves around the fundamental idea of Everything. It consists of endless Existences, unique worlds
that are separated from each other by the Nothing.

## Existence

An Existence is an individual “world” or “universe” with its own laws of physics, mythology, cultures, and species.
These worlds can be any size, from a single structure to a vast expanse of multiverses. They are defined by fundamental
Concepts that represent Existence itself. Existences are disconnected from one another, and entities within them are
generally unaware of their neighbors.

## The Nothing

In between these Existences lies the Nothing. It is not a void or abyss; it simply doesn’t exist. The opposition to
Everything is non-existent. The Nothing has a profound effect on Everything; it slowly deteriorates and corrupts
anything that comes into direct contact with it, including Everything itself.

## Concept

A Concept is a fundamental idea or system that is the foundation of an Existence. All things that exist within an
Existence are tied to its Concepts.

## Deity

A Deity is an entity that is a physical manifestation of a Concept. They are conscious beings that are aware of their
existence and are typically all-powerful and all-knowing. Deities are common throughout The Everything, but Concepts are
not required to have a Deity to represent them.

## Arch Being

An Arch Being is a powerful entity that can travel between Existences. They are defined by their Arch Ability, a unique
ability used primarily to traverse the Nothing with relative safety. An Arch Being can only possess one Arch Ability.
These entities are extremely rare, with only a few existing within a given Existence.
